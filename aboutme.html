about me


i was a monster.

a slayer of innocent succulents

they shriveled and yellowed with neglect and underwatering.

they turned to mush when i overoverwatered. i was the black thumb

but now, with some research on the internet, i have transformed my self into a caregiver of these forgiving plants

you too can turn over a new leaf and not kill your succulents

don't do a murderin'
